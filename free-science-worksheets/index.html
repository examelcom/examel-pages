About Our Science Worksheets

Our science worksheets introduce students in Grades 1-6 to the natural world through engaging, standards-aligned activities spanning life science, earth science, and physical science.

Skills covered include observation, classification, the scientific method, ecosystems, weather, matter, forces, and the human body.

Our worksheets use verified facts from trusted sources — NASA, USDA, USGS, and NOAA — to make learning accurate and engaging.
