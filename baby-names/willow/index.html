About Willow

Willow has such a graceful, flowing quality—it practically sways when you say it aloud. There's something naturally peaceful about the name, evoking images of gentle branches and quiet riverside moments. It strikes that perfect balance between feeling whimsical and grounded, modern yet timeless.

The nickname options are lovely too. You might call her Wil, Lowe, or simply use the full name, which never feels too formal. Willow tends to suit creative, thoughtful children—there's an artistic sensibility woven right into the sound of it.

It's a name that's become beloved by many families without feeling overdone. If you're drawn to nature-inspired names with a soft elegance and a touch of magic, Willow is absolutely worth considering.

According to SSA data, Willow has been given to 59,035 babies since 1975. Its most popular year was 2021 with 5,193 births.
