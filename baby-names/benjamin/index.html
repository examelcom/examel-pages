About Benjamin

Benjamin has a wonderfully substantial feel—it's a name that grows right along with your son. It carries classic charm without feeling stuffy, and there's something inherently friendly about the way it rolls off the tongue. The natural nickname Ben gives you flexibility too, letting him choose his own style as he gets older.

This is a name with real staying power. It's been beloved for generations, yet it never feels dated or tired. There's a quiet confidence to Benjamin—it works equally well on a thoughtful kid, an athlete, a creative type, or a natural leader. Parents love it because it's familiar enough that everyone will spell it right, but distinctive enough that it doesn't feel like a compromise choice.

If you're drawn to names that are both timeless and warm, Benjamin delivers on both counts. It's the kind of name that simply works.

According to SSA data, Benjamin has been given to 658,423 babies since 1975. Its most popular year was 1989 with 15,733 births.
