About William

William has a timeless, strong sound that feels both classic and approachable. There's something reassuring about it—solid and dependable, yet never stuffy. It rolls off the tongue naturally and works beautifully across different ages, from a curious toddler to a confident adult.

This name offers wonderful flexibility. Will, Liam, Bill, and Wills are all popular nicknames, giving your son options as he grows. Interestingly, Liam has surged in recent years as a standalone choice, but William keeps its steady appeal, ranking high year after year.

The personality William evokes is one of quiet confidence and kindness. It's a name that feels like it could belong to anyone—a creative dreamer, a thoughtful leader, or someone genuine and grounded. Whether you choose to use the full name or embrace a nickname, William gives your son a name with real staying power.

According to SSA data, William has been given to 1,001,496 babies since 1975. Its most popular year was 1980 with 25,638 births.
