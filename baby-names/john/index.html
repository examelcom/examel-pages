About John

John has a timeless, straightforward quality that feels both classic and genuinely grounded. It's a name that rolls off the tongue easily—just one syllable, strong and clear. There's something reassuring about how familiar it is; it's been a beloved choice for generations, yet it never feels dated or heavy.

The nickname possibilities are wonderful too. You get Jack, Johnny, or Jon depending on your style and your son's personality as he grows. John works beautifully at any age, from little boy to grown man, adapting naturally to different seasons of life.

There's an understated confidence to John. It doesn't demand attention, but it commands respect. It's the kind of name that lets a person become whoever they're meant to be—solid foundation, infinite possibility.

According to SSA data, John has been given to 1,038,114 babies since 1975. Its most popular year was 1979 with 35,412 births.
