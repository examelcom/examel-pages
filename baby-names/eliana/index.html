About Eliana

Eliana has a beautiful, flowing sound that feels both elegant and approachable. The name carries a lyrical quality with its soft vowel endings, making it pleasant to say aloud. It strikes a lovely balance between classic and contemporary—timeless enough to age well, yet fresh enough to feel current.

This is a name with real personality. Eliana evokes warmth and brightness, with a gentle sophistication that works beautifully across different life stages. Common nicknames like Eli, Liana, or Anna give your daughter options as she grows.

It's a name that's gained wonderful momentum in recent years, which means it's clearly resonating with modern parents. If you love names that sound graceful without feeling fussy, Eliana might be just right for your family.

According to SSA data, Eliana has been given to 67,724 babies since 1975. Its most popular year was 2024 with 6,423 births.
