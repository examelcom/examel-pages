About Logan

Logan has a strong, confident sound that feels both classic and contemporary. It's got a great rhythm—short, punchy, and easy to call across a playground. The name carries a grounded, approachable vibe that works equally well on a little boy and a grown man.

There's something inherently friendly about Logan. It evokes a sense of adventure and reliability, with just enough edge to feel modern. The name became hugely popular over the past couple decades, which speaks to its universal appeal—parents love how naturally it fits.

Nicknames are pretty limited (Lo works in a pinch), which is nice if you want to stick with the full name. Logan tends to suit kids who are easygoing and genuine, with a hint of mischief. It's the kind of name that feels familiar without being overused in most circles.

According to SSA data, Logan has been given to 378,162 babies since 1975. Its most popular year was 2007 with 15,330 births.
