About Amelia

Amelia has such a lovely, flowing sound—graceful without being frilly. It rolls off the tongue easily, making it a name that works beautifully across all ages, from toddler to adult. There's something timeless about it; it feels both classic and fresh at the same time.

The name carries a naturally warm personality. Amelias tend to evoke intelligence, kindness, and a quiet confidence. It's elegant without trying too hard, and it pairs wonderfully with almost any middle name or last name you might have in mind.

If you love the name but want something slightly different, cute nicknames like Amelia, Melly, Mia, or Lia give you flexibility. It's a name that gives your daughter room to grow into her own style while standing on its own as something genuinely special.

According to SSA data, Amelia has been given to 216,744 babies since 1975. Its most popular year was 2021 with 13,032 births.
