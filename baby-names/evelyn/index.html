About Evelyn

Evelyn has such a lovely, flowing sound—it glides off the tongue with natural grace. There's something both timeless and gently modern about it, striking that perfect balance between classic and contemporary. The name feels sophisticated without being stuffy, and it carries a warmth that makes it approachable and genuine.

This is a name with real personality. Evelyn evokes someone thoughtful and poised, yet still playful and down-to-earth. It's the kind of name that grows beautifully with a child—pretty and sweet for a little girl, elegant for a teenager, and substantial for an adult. Common nicknames like Evie and Eve give her options to express different sides of herself.

Evelyn has experienced a wonderful resurgence in recent years, loved by parents who appreciate its vintage charm and timeless appeal. If you're drawn to names that feel both grounded and graceful, Evelyn is a wonderful choice.

According to SSA data, Evelyn has been given to 204,551 babies since 1975. Its most popular year was 2017 with 10,747 births.
