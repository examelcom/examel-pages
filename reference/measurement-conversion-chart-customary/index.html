Measurement Conversion Chart — US Customary

Free printable US customary measurement conversion chart. Length, weight, and capacity conversions.

Length

1 foot (ft)=12 inches (in)
1 yard (yd)=3 feet (ft)
1 yard (yd)=36 inches (in)
1 mile (mi)=5,280 feet (ft)
1 mile (mi)=1,760 yards (yd)

Weight

1 pound (lb)=16 ounces (oz)
1 ton=2,000 pounds (lb)

Capacity

1 cup (c)=8 fluid ounces (fl oz)
1 pint (pt)=2 cups (c)
1 quart (qt)=2 pints (pt)
1 gallon (gal)=4 quarts (qt)
1 gallon (gal)=16 cups (c)

How to use: Print this chart and keep it at your desk or in your binder. Use it as a quick reference while solving math problems. Teachers: laminate for classroom use.
