How Image Resizing Works

Our resizer uses high-quality Lanczos resampling to resize your image while preserving sharpness and detail. You specify the desired width, and the height is calculated automatically to maintain the original aspect ratio (unless you specify both dimensions).

Common Resize Dimensions

Social media profile photos (400×400), website hero images (1920×1080), email headers (600×200), thumbnails (150×150), and presentation images (1280×720) are among the most frequently requested sizes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will resizing reduce quality?

Enlarging an image beyond its original dimensions will reduce quality. Reducing dimensions preserves quality while creating a smaller file.
